2025 Grocery Assistance Program

Important: Limited enrollment period for the 2025 Grocery Assistance Program. Apply for benefits today.
Pre-Qualified: Based on your location in Enterprise, Nevada, United States, you meet the initial requirements for this program.

New Federal Program: Qualifying individuals and families in Enterprise, Nevada can now receive up to $500 per month to help with grocery expenses as part of a new federal program. This initiative aims to support residents by easing the financial burden of food costs and promoting better nutrition.

Up to $500 Monthly Grocery Benefit

Receive up to $500 each month to purchase groceries.

Benefits are provided on a reloadable card accepted at participating retailers.

Apply for Grocery Assistance

Free • No Obligation • Secure Application

How the Program Works

  • Eligible residents of Enterprise, Nevada are pre-qualified.
  • Monthly benefits are loaded onto a secure payment card.
  • Use the card at authorized grocery stores and supermarkets.
  • Purchase nutritious foods, including fruits, vegetables, and other staples.
  • Benefits are renewed monthly for eligible participants.

What Can You Buy?

  • Fresh and frozen fruits and vegetables
  • Meat, poultry, and fish
  • Dairy products and alternatives
  • Bread, grains, and cereals
  • Non-alcoholic beverages
  • Other essential grocery items

Upon approval, your benefits card will be mailed to your address in Enterprise, Nevada, and will be ready for immediate use at participating locations.
