New Federal Program: Qualifying individuals and families can now receive up to $500 per month to help with grocery expenses as part of their health coverage benefits. The Grocery Assistance Program helps eligible Americans afford healthy food and groceries. The benefit is designed to reduce the financial burden of food costs while promoting better nutrition and health outcomes.

$500 Monthly Grocery Benefit

Receive up to $500 each month for groceries

Loaded onto a benefits card for use at participating retailers

How the Program Works

  • Monthly benefits loaded onto a program card
  • Use at participating grocery stores and supermarkets
  • Purchase healthy foods including fruits, vegetables, dairy, meat, and more
  • No restrictions on brand choices
  • Benefits do not expire within the month

What Can You Buy?

  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Meat, poultry, and fish
  • Dairy products
  • Bread and cereals
  • Snack foods and non-alcoholic beverages
  • Seeds and plants that produce food

Once approved, your benefits card will arrive within 7-10 business days, and you can start using your grocery benefits immediately.
